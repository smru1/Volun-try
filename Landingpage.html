About us, the VolunTry project


Who we are!

Anna, Smru, Susie and Manuela have joined forces for this amazing project organised by Code First: Girls! Besides their desire to learn more about coding, they are also passionate about volunteering.

Volun-try is a local business which aims to help people find exciting volunteering opportunities they may not previously have discovered under one roof. Volun-try provides the chance to explore opportunities which appeal to a variety of passions and interests, including:
  • Animal Shelters
  • Charity Shops
  • Elderly Care
  • Food Banks
  • And much more...

Volun-try offers rewarding volunteering opportunities that really make a long-lasting positive impact in our society. If you have spare time and are keen to volunteer then take a look at the opportunities in your area today and help make a positive change!
