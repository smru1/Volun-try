Helping The Charity Shops

Charity shops are always on the look out for volunteers of all ages, and there are many chairty shop organisations that support a variety of different causes to choose from, so to participate please see below opportunities to help your local charity shops

OXFAM

Oxfam is a charity that is fighting global poverty and is globally renowned for aid and development with 70 years of experience, working and campaigning with partners in over 90 countries worldwide.

CANCER RESEARCH UK

Cancer Research is an organisation that helps to fund scientists, doctors and nurses to help beat cancer sooner whilst also providing cancer information and support to the public.

THE SALVATION ARMY

The Salvation Army is a Protestant Christian church and an international charitable relief agency that provides aids after natural or man-made disasters. They have worked to alleviate suffering and help people rebuild their lives.

BRITISH RED CROSS

The British Red Cross helps people in crisis, whoever and wherever they are by responding to natural disasters, conflicts and individual emergencies.

SUE RYDER

Sue Ryder supports people through the most difficult times of their lives. For over 65 years their doctors, nurses and carers have given people the compassion and expert care they need to help them live the best life they possibly can.

BARNADO'S

Barnado's is a british charity that supports vulnerable children.
