Feeding the Hungry

London has food banks and soup kitchens all over the city. Whether you're interested in donating the food itself or dedicating your time to serving it, these places are here to help you help others.

BRIXTON SOUP KITCKEN

A Brixton-based service for people from London and the surrounding areas who are experiencing homelessness or are otherwise in need.

CRISIS CAFE

Café from Crisis works to end homelessness through practical hospitality training whist serving fresh and socially conscious food and coffee in our bustling cafés.

FOOD CYCLE

Food Cycle supports people who are hungry and lonely by serving tasty lunches and dinners every single day in towns and cities across the country.

STREET LYTES

Street Lytes aims to ensure that homeless and vulnerable people are able to meet their practical and emotional needs, and to move towards housing, employment and happiness in the future.

THE UPPER ROOM

The Upper Room supports people who are hungry and lonely by serving tasty lunches and dinners every single day in towns and cities across the country.

TRUSSEL TRUST

Trussel Trust facilitates financial and food donations, corporate partnerships, fundraising challenges and volunteering opportunities all across the UK.
